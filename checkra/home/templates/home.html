What is Checkra?

Checkra is an information aggregator and extractor that currently works on podcasts, and will scale to many more information sources including financial data, books, and research papers.
It'll cross-reference this data to mainstream news and social media, and with ML-classifiers, Checkra will be able to detect misinformation at its inception.


Current Features
  • Explore pre-processed podcasts from Lex Fridman, available at "Podcast Breakdowns"
  • Visualize which speakers mentioned specific entities, availabe at "Entity Graphs"

Future Updates
  • Scale podcast library to more podcasts and podcasters
  • Implement book processing
  • Allow users to process their own text
  • Monitor mentions of companies and stocks
  • Detect social media misinformation trends
