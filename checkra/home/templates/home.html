What is Checkra?

Checkra is an information aggregator and extractor that currently gathers information from podcasts, and will scale to many more information sources including books, blogs and research papers.
It'll cross-reference this data to mainstream news and social media, and with ML-classifiers, Checkra will be able to detect misinformation at its inception.
There will be a finance component to monitor rising stocks and markets, and automatically provide due diligence from information sources.
Currently using podcast transcripts from HappyScribe, so huge shoutout to them.


Current Features
  • Explore pre-processed podcasts from "Lex Fridman" and "The Investor Podcast", available at the "Podcast Breakdowns" tab
  • Visualize which speakers mentioned specific entities, availabe at the "Entity Graphs" tab

Future Updates
  • Scale podcast library to more podcasts and podcasters
  • Implement book processing
  • Allow users to process their own text
  • Monitor mentions of companies and stocks
