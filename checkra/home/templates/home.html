What is Checkra?

Checkra is an information aggregator that currently works on podcasts, and will scale to many more sources including financial data, books, and research papers.
It'll cross-reference this data to mainstream news and social media, and with ML-classifiers, Checkra will be able to detect misinformation at its inception.


Current Features
  • Explore pre-processed podcasts from Lex Fridman
  • Visualize which speakers mentioned specific entities

Future Updates
  • Scale podcast library to more podcasts
  • Implement book processing
  • Allow users to process their own text
  • Monitor mentions of companies and stocks
  • Detect social media misinformation trends
